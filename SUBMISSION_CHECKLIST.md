## How To Run Experiments

Small smoke run:

```powershell
python src/experiments.py --folders sm_j10 sm_j20 --approaches topo_seq id_ssgs lft_ssgs ga --time-budget 10 --workers 1 --limit 10
```

Full run:

```powershell
./run_experiments.ps1 -TimeBudget 28 -Workers 1
```

Artifacts are written to `experiments/<timestamp>/`:
- `machine_specs.json`
- `results_per_instance.csv`
- `results_aggregate.csv`
- `summary.md`
